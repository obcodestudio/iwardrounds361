 Description:
     The Caring Ward Rounds Management App streamlines daily patient monitoring with a focus on speed and accuracy. It allows nursing staff to easily record vital signs—such as temperature, blood pressure, and glucose levels—while minimizing entry errors through a simplified interface. Featuring a built-in medical glossary for rapid annotation and a hassle-free data import system, the app ensures patient care is both safe and efficient.
 Functions:
 Nurse maintenance.
 Patient maintenance.
 Phrase maintenance.
 System settings, weight units, body temperature units.
 Daily report list.
 To record your systolic, diastolic, pulse rate, important information, body temperature, weight, blood oxygen level, blood sugar level.
 Data transmission with e-mail.
 Supports four data export formats: TXT, CSV, XML, and JSON.
