 Description:
     Caring ward rounds management app promptly, smoothly, accurately and comprehensively demonstrates the mobile medical treatment software style. Nursing staff has to conduct daily physiological measurements on each hospital bed patient every day, with the combination of all functional characteristics, the physiological parameters including body temperature, blood pressure, blood sugar, blood oxygen and weight, as well as several documentation items such as patient annotation assist you in watching over each of the inputted data. It may be seen everywhere, and the simpler control items together with the single input structure reduces the occurrence of typo mistakes from the personnel. The smooth transition between the different items offers convenience and safety. The phrase function simplifies annotation input by directly introducing technical terms often used in medical treatment, allowing you fast access to the desired content. For import services, all you need to do is to send the computer data on nursing staff, patient or medical treatment technical terms via e-mail to iPad, then use the select all and copy functions to paste to the import section of the software in order to complete the data transfer with ease.
